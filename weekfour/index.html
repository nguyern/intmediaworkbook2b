<h3> he got me flowers!!! he doesn't know that i prefer peonies though </h3>
<p> This p5.js sketch was inspired by Yayoi Kusama's art exhibition and her flower room. I've duplicated the flowers over and over again to completely cover the page. The orginial text says "roses are red, wait do you even like flowers?" in an attempt to make fun of the romantic gesture when it has no thought behind it. Flowers are a beautiful gesture of affection but can be meaningless when not thought about properly. Almost how like my crush got me all these flowers but I don't think he realises my favourite flower are peonies and I don't really like red. </p>
